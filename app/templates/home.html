Welcome to the visualisation tool for trend analysis of Flickr tags.

In this visualisation tool, trends of tags can be explored on a temporal and spatial bases. The data used in this visualisation originates from a Flickr dataset with images and meta data for each image. Tags from these images are binned per time step (three months), country and category. For each bin the top 5 most popular tags are shown to follow the trends over time and space. The device and its brand name that was used to capture these images were also added to be able to have a look at how the use of these change over time.

Features of the visualisation tool:

  • Coloured world map based on most popular category
  • Mouseover country for more information
  • Filter data on category
  • Compare two countries by clicking country
  • Timeline with popularity of each category over time
  • Selectable timeframe
  • Automated play true the whole timeline
  • Camera device and brand usage
  • Top 5 most popular tags
